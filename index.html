Forbidden Names in Islam

Names that are discouraged in Islam due to their meanings or associations

In Islam, certain names are considered forbidden (haram) or highly discouraged (makruh) for various reasons:

  • Names that are exclusive to Allah (e.g., Al-Ahad, Al-Khaliq)
  • Names that imply worship to other than Allah
  • Names with negative or unpleasant meanings
  • Names that may cause embarrassment or mockery
  • Names of tyrants or famous evil personalities
